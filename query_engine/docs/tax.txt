### Understanding TDS (Tax Deducted at Source)
* **What is TDS?**: TDS is a mechanism where tax is deducted at the time of payment by the payer, and the balance amount is paid to the recipient. The deducted tax is then remitted to the government.
* **Common Scenarios for TDS**: Salaries, interest income (e.g., from Fixed Deposits), rent payments, professional fees, and contract payments.
* **TDS Certificates**: Payers are required to issue TDS certificates (e.g., Form 16 for salaries, Form 16A for non-salary payments) to recipients, which can be used to claim credit for the tax deducted.
* **How FinCove Helps with TDS**:
    * **Viewing TDS details**: Link to view TDS deducted on your FinCove bank accounts (e.g., interest on FDs) through integrated portals.
    * [cite_start]**Form 15G/15H submission**: Easily submit Form 15G/15H through the FinCove app to avoid TDS on interest income if eligible[cite: 26].
    * **Reconciling TDS**: Guidance on how to match TDS entries in your Form 26AS with your income records.

### Advance Tax Explained
* **Who Needs to Pay Advance Tax?**: Individuals and businesses whose estimated tax liability for the financial year (after deducting TDS) exceeds a certain threshold (currently ₹10,000).
* **Due Dates for Advance Tax Installments**:
    * June 15th (15% of tax liability)
    * September 15th (45% of tax liability)
    * December 15th (75% of tax liability)
    * March 15th (100% of tax liability)
* **Penalties for Non-Payment/Short Payment**: Interest under Sections 234B and 234C of the Income Tax Act.
* [cite_start]**How FinCove Facilitates Advance Tax Payments**: Direct link to the e-pay tax portal for seamless advance tax payments[cite: 22].

### GST E-Invoicing (for Businesses & Traders)
* **What is E-Invoicing?**: E-invoicing is a system where B2B invoices are electronically authenticated by the GSTN (GST Network) for further use on the common GST portal.
* **Applicability**: Mandatory for businesses exceeding a certain aggregate turnover threshold.
* **Benefits**: Reduces data entry errors, simplifies GST return filing, and improves compliance.
* **FinCove's Role (Potential Feature/Integration)**: Future integration with e-invoicing solutions to help businesses generate and manage e-invoices directly or through partnered platforms.
